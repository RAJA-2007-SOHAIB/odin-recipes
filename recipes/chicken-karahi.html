Chicken-Karahi

descripsion

This dish gets its name from a karahi, a flat open pot used a lot in Pakistani and Indian cooking. In spite of its name, this recipe can be made in any pot. it's a little spicy (by Pakistani standards), but simple and foolproof.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sunflower oil
  • 1 whole chicken, giblets discarded, cut these into 12 pieces
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 ginger and garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoons red chile powder
  • 1/4 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 6 ripe tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 4 green chile peppers, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch fresh cilanto leaves, finely chopped
