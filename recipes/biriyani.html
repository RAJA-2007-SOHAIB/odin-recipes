biryani


Description

Biriyani is a beloved South Asian rice dish renowned for its rich aroma, vibrant colors, and layered spices. Often considered Pakistan's national dish, it combines fragrant basmati rice with marinated meat, saffron, and caramelized onions, slow-cooked to perfection. Every region adds its own twist—be it Sindhi, Hyderabadi, or Karachi-style—making biryani not just a meal, but a cultural identity. Served at weddings, festivals, and daily dinners alike, it unites people through tradition and taste. Its global popularity continues to grow, admired for both its flavor and heritage.
